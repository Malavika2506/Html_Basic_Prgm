Cybersquare learning platform

Every registered student will have a separate login account to the Cyber Square online learning platform. Here, they will be able to view their course progress, assignments provided by teachers, their batch performance, progress cards, and so on. They will be able to attend oline exams and live classes. Students can attend live classes to clear their doubts and interact with teachers.Apart from these, they will also get notifications on upcoming live classes, news, and events so that won't miss them.

Teachers can also login into cybersquare platform and able to view the students performance, batch progress, make reports, evaluate their works such as assignments, projects, questions, etc. Teachers can schedule an online class and online exams also.
